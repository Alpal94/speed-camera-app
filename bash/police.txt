<h1 id="main-desc" >Camera Locations</h1>


    
    
        <span class="contentText">
            <p>Western Australia roads are covered by a network of fixed and mobile speed cameras. They are located on our high risk roads and intersections to prevent speed and red-light offences. The Western Australia Police is happy for drivers to be aware of most speed camera locations to encourage them to slow down and try to prevent a serious or fatal collision.</p>
<p>Mobile speed locations are published online weekly, in newspapers and broadcast on numerous radio stations. Current fixed site locations are listed below. Locations are correct at time of publication but may change without notice.</p>
